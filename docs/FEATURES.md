# Feature Set & Social Impact — CivicGuide India

CivicGuide India is more than a chatbot—it is a comprehensive civic education toolkit.

---

## 🌟 Core Feature Catalog

### 1. AI Electoral Assistant
- **Real-time Answers**: Questions about registration, voting rights, and ECI rules.
- **Agentic Research**: Search for candidate affidavits and election timelines.
- **Multilingual UI**: Native support for 6 major Indian languages.

### 2. Rumor Verifier (Fact-Check)
- **Claim Analysis**: Direct pasting of suspected misinformation.
- **Official Verdicts**: Structured debunking using official ECI data.
- **Neutrality**: Balanced analysis that avoids political bias.

### 3. EVM & VVPAT Simulator
- **Interactive Guide**: Visual walkthrough of the voting process.
- **VVPAT Verification**: Explains the 7-second slip confirmation rule.
- **Accessibility**: Optimized for keyboard and screen reader users.

### 4. Polling Station Locator
- **Address Search**: Powered by Google Maps Places Autocomplete.
- **Geolocation**: "Find My Nearest" booth functionality.
- **Accessibility**: Includes text-based directions for screen reader users.

---

## 👥 User Personas Served

1. **The First-Time Voter (Age 18-20)**: Focuses on Form 6 registration and "How to Vote" guides.
2. **The Senior Citizen (Age 80+)**: Focuses on "Home Voting" (Form 12D) and facility information.
3. **The PWD Voter**: Focuses on booth accessibility and Saksham ECI facilities.
4. **The NRI Voter**: Focuses on Form 6A and postal ballot rules.

---

## 🌍 Social Impact Goals
- **Reduce Voter Apathy**: By making information accessible and encouraging.
- **Combat Misinformation**: Providing a trusted alternative to unverified social media news.
- **Increase Transparency**: Directing users to official candidate affidavits and manifestos.
- **Digital Inclusion**: Bringing complex electoral rules to the fingertips of every smartphone user.
